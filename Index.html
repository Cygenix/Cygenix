How it works
Four steps from server to cloud

Cygenix connects to your existing infrastructure, uses Claude to intelligently map and validate your data, then migrates it safely to your cloud target of choice.

1

Connect your source

Point Cygenix at your on-premise file server, NAS, or legacy storage. No firewall changes needed — we use a lightweight agent.

2

AI analyses your data

Claude scans your folder structures, file types, and metadata — building an intelligent migration map automatically.

3

Review & approve

Your IT team reviews the proposed migration plan in a clear dashboard. Adjust targets, tag priorities, flag sensitive data.

4

Migrate & verify

Cygenix executes the migration with real-time progress tracking and post-migration integrity verification on every file.

Features
Built for IT teams who care about reliability

Every feature is designed around the real concerns of enterprise IT — auditability, security, and zero data loss.

AI-powered schema mapping

Claude understands your folder taxonomy and automatically maps source structures to your target cloud hierarchy — including renaming conventions and metadata tagging.

Zero-downtime migration

Cygenix runs delta syncs so your team keeps working during migration. Files are cut over only once fully verified at the destination.

Integrity verification

Every migrated file is checksum-verified against the source. Cygenix produces a full audit log suitable for compliance reporting and ISO audits.

PII & sensitive data detection

Claude flags files likely to contain personal data, financial records or health information — so your DPO can review before migration completes.

Multi-cloud targets

Migrate to AWS S3, Azure Blob Storage, Google Cloud Storage, SharePoint, or any S3-compatible endpoint from a single unified platform.

Plain-English reporting

After each migration run, Claude generates a readable summary for management — no raw logs, no jargon, just clear status and any items needing attention.
