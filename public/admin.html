Invite Users
Send access invitations to testers and clients
ℹ️ Invitations are sent directly from Netlify Identity. Click the button above to open the Netlify Identity panel, then click Invite users and enter the email address. The user receives an email with a link to set their password and access cygenix.co.uk.
How to invite a user — 3 steps
1
Open Netlify Identity
Click Open Netlify Identity ↗ above, or go to app.netlify.com → your site → Identity tab
2
Click "Invite users"
Enter the user's email address. You can invite multiple at once by entering one per line.
3
User receives invite email
They get an email from Netlify with a link to set their password. Once done, they can log in at cygenix.co.uk/login.html. Their record will appear in the Users tab here after first login.
